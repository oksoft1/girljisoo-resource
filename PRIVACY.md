# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 1, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how this Telegram AI Bot ("the Bot", "we", "our") handles, stores, and processes user data. This Bot is designed as a personal AI companion project that utilizes Firebase for long-term memory and large language models (LLMs) for generating text responses.

By interacting with the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized conversational experience, the Bot collects and processes the following data:

* **Telegram User Data:** Your unique Telegram User ID (`chat.id` or `user.id`). We **do not** collect your real name, phone number, or Telegram username unless explicitly provided by you in the chat.
* **Chat History:** The text messages you send to the Bot and the replies generated by the AI are temporarily stored to maintain conversational context.
* **User Profile Memory (Long-Term Memory):** Key facts, preferences, and entities extracted from your conversations (e.g., likes, dislikes, recent status) are stored to maintain long-term consistency.

---

## 2. How Your Data Is Processed and Stored

We prioritize data optimization and privacy through the following architectural practices:

* **Database Storage:** All collected data (Chat History and User Profile Memory) is stored securely in a **Firebase Realtime Database**.
* **Memory Pruning (Sliding Window):** To optimize performance and privacy, older chat messages are periodically summarized and permanently deleted from the active chat history. 
* **Third-Party AI APIs:** Text inputs are sent securely to third-party AI providers (such as Google Gemini, Groq, Anthropic Claude, or Hugging Face) solely for response generation. These platforms process the data in accordance with their respective API privacy policies (which generally state that API data is not used to train public models).

---

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

* We only retain your chat logs and profile data for as long as necessary to provide the conversational AI service.
* **Data Deletion:** If you wish to wipe your memory or delete all data associated with your Telegram User ID from our Firebase Database, you can request data deletion at any time (or manually clear it if a `/clear` command is implemented).

---

## 4. Security of Your Data

The security of your data is important to us. We use standard secure protocols (HTTPS/WSS) to transmit data between Telegram, Vercel (our hosting provider), Firebase, and the AI APIs. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this `PRIVACY.md` file.

---

## 6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request the complete deletion of your data, please contact the repository owner/developer directly via Telegram or GitHub.
